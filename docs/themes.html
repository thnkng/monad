Monad Themes

Philosophy: Themes are not mere color swaps — they are emotional atmospheres.

Each theme embodies a distinct mindset, designed for different contexts and moods.

Available Themes

Four carefully crafted emotional palettes. Choose the atmosphere that matches your work.

Default

Soft clarity — Pure white with subtle warmth

Calm, serene, effortless reading. GitHub Light inspired.

Usage

Apply a theme by adding the theme attribute to your <body> tag:

Moonlight (Default)

<body>
  <!-- No attribute needed -->
</body>

Midnight

<body theme="midnight">
  <!-- Dark theme -->
</body>

Ocean

<body theme="ocean">
  <!-- Modern cyan/teal theme -->
</body>

Purple

<body theme="purple">
  <!-- Elegant violet theme -->
</body>

Design Philosophy

Emotional Precision

Each theme is not just a color palette — it's an emotional atmosphere carefully crafted to evoke specific feelings and support different types of work.

Semantic Integrity

All themes maintain the same semantic structure. Success is always success, danger is always danger — only the emotional tone shifts.

Accessibility First

Every theme meets WCAG AA contrast requirements. Beauty never comes at the cost of readability.
